Help

xRate is a small and simple web app to convert the exchagne currency for travellers.

As you input numbers, the results will show automatically.

In Settings, you can check Access Location so that the currency you are using will appear at the top of the home page.

In Edit, you can add or remove the currency in the home page.

In Themes, xRate provides six themes for you to choose.
